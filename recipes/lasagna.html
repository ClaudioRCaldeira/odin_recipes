Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 minutes.
  3. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hour.
